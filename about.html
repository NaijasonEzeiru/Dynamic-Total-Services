About Total Dynamic Services

At Dynamic Total Services, we strongly believe in unlocking potential, one opportunity at a time, empowering individuals to embrace their greatest and shape their future. We take pride in being a labour hire company that specialises in providing highly skilled candidates to work on mining sites across Western Australia. As a recruitment agency for various organisations, we provide an external Human Resources service to outsource candidates that meet the criteria for the opportunity.

We are deeply committed in offering a distinctive combination of specialised industry expertise, exceptional customer service, and a comprehensive candidate screening process. With a deep understanding of the mining and industrial sectors, we possess specialised industry knowledge that enables us to cater to the unique requirements and challenges of these industries. Our team of experienced professionals brings valuable insights and expertise, ensuring we can effectively match skilled workers to specific job roles and projects.

Our Goal

At Dynamic Total Services, our mission is to be the leading provider of skilled labour solutions in the mining, construction, and industrial sectors. We are committed to delivering exceptional value to our clients by supplying top-tier talent, fostering strong relationships, and facilitating seamless workforce solutions.

Our Values

Through our unwavering commitment to excellence, sustainable partnerships, talent empowerment, safety and compliance, and making a positive impact, Dynamic Total Services strives to be the preferred labour hire agency, delivering exceptional value to our clients, candidates, and the industries we serve.

We foster a positive work culture built on respect, collaboration, and teamwork. We prioritise open communication, value diverse perspectives, and promote a supportive environment where employees feel empowered and motivated. We celebrate achievements, encourage work-life balance, and promote employee well-being.

Our Mission

At Dynamic Total Services, our mission is to deliver unparalleled recruitment solutions driven by a steadfast commitment to Safety, Quality, and Excellence. We strive to provide a trusted partnership, ensuring the highest standards of safety in every placement we make. Our rigorous screening processes guarantee exceptional quality, matching top-notch candidates to meet the unique demands of our clients. With an unwavering pursuit of excellence, we aim to exceed expectations and become the go-to recruitment agency in the industry. Together, let's forge a future where Safety, Quality, and Excellence are the cornerstones of success.
